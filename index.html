Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

It is a quite place for students to take breather or to take their mind off of something. Some students come to read or accompany their friends who want to pray.



School Library

Was built in 1990

This is the favorite place of many bookworms of the school. There's a wide variety of genres, and visitors spend a lot of time in the library when the other studenths are in classes.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1962

This is where meetings of the school occur. For example, if the principal has to inform the students about some changes, they would set a date, and sit in the auditorium to listen to the principal. Or, if a student needs to inform everyone about something, then they would do it in this room.
